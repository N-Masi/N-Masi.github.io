SprintBox: the Future of Timing

Millisecond precision at affordable prices, say goodbye to stopwatches

What is SprintBox

SprintBox is designed primarily for short distance track & field athletes. However, it can be used by athletes of any sport. While stopwatches typically differ by 0.24s from Fully Automatic Timing that you see at meets, research shows that human variability means the real results can be anywhere from 0.07s to 0.31s off!

SprintBox gives you millisecond-precise measurements that you can use to fine-tine your training, get seed times for meets, or even get your 40y dash time if you're a football athlete looking to make it to the next level.

How it Works

You set one device at the start of your race, and at the press of a button it gives you a countdown. As soon as the buzzer goes off, you run to the finish. The device at the finish gets your exact time, and displays it on its screen.
